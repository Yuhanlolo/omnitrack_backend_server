report_problem Delete Experiment

If you delete the experiment, the following data entities will also removed:

  • The experiment information
  • Study participant information
  • OmniTrack trackers, triggers, and items which have been injected for this experiment

The following information will remain:

  • OmniTrack user account of the study participants
  • Trackers, triggers, and items of users that are not related to this experiment
  • Study participants' interaction logs

If you want to delete the experiment, insert your experiment's ID in the following form:

{{data.experimentId}}
